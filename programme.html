Food

Celebrate with your friends in a tea party inspired by Alice's adventures in Wonderland. Prepare your taste buds for macaroons, eclairs and cake, as well as the finest tea and wine.

Music

The party shall feature one of Britain’s top young accordion players – and Clare Hall’s very own – Ben de Souza, joined by star violinist Chloë Meade. Let their music bring you the wonder in Wonderland!

We are also proud to announce Eleanor Medcalf, a seasoned veteran of the National Youth Orchestra of Great Britain, will be gracing our ears with the melodious wonders of the harp at our summer soirée this July.
